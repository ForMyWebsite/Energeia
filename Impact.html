Energy Tips

Use Appliances During Off-Peak Hours

Try running your washing machine or dishwasher in the evening when electricity demand is lower. This can help save energy and money.

Turn Off Lights When Not in Use

Get into the habit of switching off lights when you leave a room. It might seem small, but it adds up over time!

Unplug Devices

Many electronics consume power even when turned off. Unplugging devices when you're not using them can save energy and reduce your bill.
